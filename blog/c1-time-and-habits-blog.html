First Thoughts

Being at the start of this journey I haven't had the opportunity to go through too many of the techniques that were advised. I am glad that the course summarises some of the methods out there because, while independent investigation is important, there are not enough minutes in a lifetime to trawl through every internet article that claims to have the secret. I am going to focus on the strategies that seem a bit more thought out, so while I have a deep love for advice listicles I will perhaps steer clear for the time being.

Three of the tips I am going to try in the next week are:

  • Meditation - thousands of monks can't be wrong
  • Pomodoro Technique - it comes highly recommended and makes conceptual sense
  • Analysing my rythmn - like a cicada I need to find out what gels and what grates

I'll keep this updated in the coming weeks.
