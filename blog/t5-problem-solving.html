Getting Blocked

In sprint 4 I was horribly blocked by one of the practice JS questions. There was something about it where I couldn't work out how the syntax was supposed to be ordered. I started with trial and error but then moved on to psuedocode but no luck.
There was fortunatley a lot of resources online and I was able to find a similar question that incorporated the same syntax which I was able to mimic and make fit.
Solving it was a huge relief, though I am glad for the struggle because it is the dread of going through that drawn-out thought process again that really cements the knowledge in your mind. I didn't learn that much in a meta sense (of course I learnt the necessary syntax to resolve the issue) but it is good to reaffirm that sticking to it will get you the results you want.
